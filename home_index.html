Overview!

Hair metal is a subgenre of heavy metal that features pop-influenced hooks and guitar riffs, upbeat rock anthems, and slow power ballads. It borrows heavily from the fashion and image of 1970s glam rock.

Most Popular Bands-

Motley Crue, Guns N Roses, Def Leppard, Poison, Bon Jovi, Ratt, Kiss, Cinderella, W.A.S.P., Whitesnake, Warrent,
